Help & Support

We read every email. Usually back within a day.

Something not working the way you expect? Stuck on the app? Need to delete your account or get a refund? Email us at hello@afletics.com and we'll sort it.

Quick answers

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Afletics → Settings → Subscription → Manage. You'll get a secure customer portal link emailed to you (this is how Apple requires it on iOS). From the portal you can cancel, switch plans, or update payment.

I lost access to my email — how do I sign in?

Email us at hello@afletics.com from a second address you can prove you control (one you've used with us before), and we'll help you recover the account. We can't reset accounts purely from inside the app — that would defeat the magic-link sign-in.

How do I change my macros / workout plan / details?

Inside the app: Nutrition tab → Edit macro goal for macros. Workout tab → swap or rebuild your plan via AI coach. Profile → tap any field on the "About you" sheet to update height, weight, goals, or training days per week.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Account → Delete account. You'll get a confirmation step. Deletion is permanent and removes every workout, food log, photo, and measurement we hold on you. Stripe subscription records are kept for the minimum period UK law requires for tax/audit purposes, then deleted.

Is my data safe?

Short version: yes. We're UK GDPR compliant, your photos and check-ins are private to you and your assigned trainer (if any), we don't sell or share data with advertisers, and we never store passwords in plaintext. The full detail lives in our Privacy Policy.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Same email — hello@afletics.com. Screenshots help. If it's urgent, say so in the subject line.

Trainer accounts — how does payout work?

Afletics uses Stripe Connect. Once you've connected your Stripe account (Settings → Stripe), client payments land in your Stripe balance and pay out to your bank on your normal Stripe schedule. We take a 5% platform fee per transaction; everything else is yours. Stripe's standard processing fees apply on top.

Still stuck?

Email hello@afletics.com. Tell us your account email, the device you're on, and what you were trying to do when it went wrong. A screenshot or screen recording is gold.
