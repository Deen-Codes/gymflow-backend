- **Macros are reference estimates** for unbranded/generic items (homemade meals, restaurant dishes without published nutrition info, traditional cuisines). Sourced from standard nutrition databases (USDA, McCance & Widdowson, BNF) and brand-published per-serving data where available.
- **Alcohol entries** under-shoot kcal vs (4P+4C+9F) macro math because ethanol provides 7 kcal/g and isn't a macronutrient — this generates 257 expected warnings, all alcohol. Not an error.
- **No `verified: false` items** were used — every entry uses standard reference data, but real-world brand variants may differ slightly. Review-worthy categories if Deen wants spot-checks: restaurant chain estimates (numbers based on typical UK serving sizes and flavour-matched analogues), homemade meal macros, less-mainstream branded items.
